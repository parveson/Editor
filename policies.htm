Review process

Generally, MSS will be assigned to two or three anonymous reviewers who have expertise related to the subject MS. The reviewers are expected to submit reviews within 30 days. A reminder will be sent after this time, and again after 60 days. If a review is not submitted within 90 days, the assignment is given to another reviewer.
